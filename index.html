Plant of The Month

The Snake Plant

A favorite for beginners and seasoned plant parents alike, the Snake Plant is virtually indestructible! Known for its striking vertical leaves and air-purifying qualities, this plant is perfect for any space, from bedrooms to offices.

Why We Love It

  • Low Maintenance: Thrives in low light and only needs to be watered every couple of weeks!
  • Air Purifier: Snake Plants remove toxins like formaldehyde and benzene from the air.
  • Pet-Friendly: Unlike many houseplants, Snake Plants are non-toxic to pets, making them safe for homes with furry friends.
